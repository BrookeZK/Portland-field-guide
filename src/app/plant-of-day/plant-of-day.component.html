  • Common Name: Twinflower
    Scientific Name: Linnaea borealis

    Plant Community: western hemlock-douglas fir forest

    Plant Type: forb

    Mature Height: 4-7 in.

    Habitat Type: forest, forest slope

    Description: little waif of a plant found throughout the northern hemisphere in circumboreal habitats. Twinflower occurs in open forests, heaths, and open dry slopes in the mountains across the northern hemisphere.
